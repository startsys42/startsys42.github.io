What is Startsys?

Startsys is a project aimed at researching a set of technologies and arts. And perhaps one day become a company dedicated to technological research, hosting and event organization.

What are those technologies?

Within the project, in addition to services and servers, it would be good to investigate topics related to robotics and home automation (drones, smart hydroponics, etc.), video games and security.

What are the next steps?

  • Setting up our own server.
  • Create social networks.
  • Study English (we will try to reach as wide an audience as possible).
  • Study topics related to audiovisuals such as sound production, film and 3D design.
  • Study video game production.
  • Study cryptography and steganography.
  • Study other disciplines that we can use for our events such as painting...

What types of events can be expected?

Well, it would be interesting, in addition to video games, to create challenges of different kinds. For example, we have pixel-war, who wants to be a millionaire, do you know more than a primary school child and ... interesting!!
