Startsys42

What is Startsys42?

Startsys42 is an interdisciplinary project exploring technology, art and creativity as well as their union. Its goal is to research different technological areas, produce audiovisual content, develop videogames and, in the future, organize events and offer own services.

Project areas

Audiovisual

Production of videos, clips, animations and creative content.

Videogames

Development of experimental, educational and entertainment videogames.

Robotics and Home Automation

Prototypes, automation, drones, intelligent systems and hydroponics.

Security and Cryptography

Examples, tools, encryption and steganography techniques.

Art and Creativity

Exploration of artistic processes, design, 3D, painting and new forms of interaction.

Project goals

  • Research emerging technologies.
  • Develop functional prototypes.
  • Build tools and educational content.
  • Create a community around art and technology.
  • Prepare the foundations for a future company dedicated to research, hosting and events.

Next steps

  • Set up our own server and web services.
  • Create presence on social networks.
  • Enhance language learning (especially English).
  • Study audiovisual production: sound, cinema, 3D modeling and animation.
  • Develop our own videogames.
  • Continue learning cryptography and steganography.
  • Research disciplines useful for future events.

What events can we expect?

Startsys42 seeks to organize technological, creative and social events. Some ideas:

  • Interactive challenges and contests.
  • Videogame-based activities.
  • Knowledge games like "Who Wants to Be a Millionaire?" or "Are You Smarter Than a 5th Grader?".
  • Collaborative dynamics like pixel war.
  • Workshops and exhibitions of art and technology.
