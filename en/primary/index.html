Startsys42

What is Startsys42?

Startsys42 is an interdisciplinary project exploring technology, art, and creativity. Its goal is to research different technological areas, produce audiovisual content, develop video games, and in the future, organize events and offer its own services. We are passionate about intellectual challenges, puzzles, and creating interactive experiences that combine riddles, technology, and fun.

Project Areas

Audiovisual

Production of videos, clips, animations, and creative content.

Video Games

Development of experimental, educational, and entertainment video games.

Robotics & Automation

Prototypes, automation, drones, intelligent systems, and hydroponics.

Security & Cryptography

Examples, tools, encryption techniques, and steganography. We will create capture-the-flag challenges, cryptography puzzles, and educational games.

Art & Creativity

Exploration of artistic processes, design, 3D, painting, and new forms of interaction.

Project Objectives

  • Research emerging technologies.
  • Develop functional prototypes.
  • Create educational tools and content.
  • Build a community around art and technology.
  • Prepare the foundation for a future company dedicated to research, hosting, and events.

Next Steps

  • Set up our own server and web services.
  • Create presence on social networks.
  • Enhance language learning, especially English.
  • Study audiovisual production: sound, film, 3D modeling, and animation.
  • Develop our own video games.
  • Continue learning cryptography and steganography.
  • Research disciplines useful for future events.

What Events Can We Expect?

Startsys42 aims to organize technological, creative, and social events. Some ideas include:

  • Interactive challenges and contests.
  • Video game-based activities.
  • Knowledge games like "Who Wants to Be a Millionaire?"
  • Collaborative dynamics like pixel wars.
  • Workshops and exhibitions in art and technology.
